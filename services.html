01

AC Repair & Servicing

Repairs · Cleaning · Gas refill · Installation

AC not cooling? Strange noise, water dripping, or a unit that just gave up in the middle of summer? Our AC technicians work on split units, ducted systems and central AC, and most repairs happen the same day you call.

  • Same-day AC repair and fault-finding
  • AC cleaning and servicing for cooler, healthier air
  • Gas refills and compressor repairs
  • Duct cleaning to remove dust and odours
  • New AC installation and yearly maintenance plans

02

Plumbing Services

Leaks · Drains · Water heaters · Bathrooms

Whether it's a dripping tap, a hidden leak, a blocked drain or a full bathroom upgrade, our plumbers fix it properly the first time and leave everything clean and dry.

  • Leak detection and repair, including hidden pipe leaks
  • Blocked drains and toilets cleared fast
  • Taps, showers, toilets and sinks installed or replaced
  • Water heater installation and repair
  • Water filters and pumps supplied and fitted

03

Painting & Wallpaper

Interiors · Exteriors · Feature walls · Wallpaper

A fresh coat changes everything. Our painters fill, sand and prime before the first coat goes on, so the finish looks great and lasts. Furniture covered, edges crisp, and no paint where it shouldn't be.

  • Room, apartment and villa painting, inside and out
  • Feature walls and textured finishes
  • Wallpaper hanging with invisible joins
  • Anti-mould treatments for bathrooms and humid rooms

04

Carpentry & Woodwork

Wardrobes · Doors · Wood floors · Custom pieces

Real carpenters who measure twice and cut once. Whether it's a built-in wardrobe, a wooden floor or a one-off custom piece, it's made to fit your space exactly.

  • Custom wardrobes, cabinets and shelving
  • Doors hung, repaired and locks replaced
  • Solid wood and parquet flooring, fitted and restored
  • Furniture repairs and restoration
  • Decorative woodwork and custom engraving

05

Tiling & Flooring

Marble · Granite · Ceramic · Bathrooms & kitchens

Tiling done right is a craft. Our tilers have laid floors across half of Dubai, and you can see it in the straight lines, level floors and neat grout. Marble, granite, ceramic or large-format tiles, indoors and out.

  • Floor and wall tiling for any room
  • Marble, granite and ceramic laid with perfect alignment
  • Bathroom and kitchen tiling, including waterproofing
  • Cracked tile replacement and regrouting

06

Ceilings, Partitions & Plastering

Gypsum ceilings · Room dividers · Wall repairs

Want a modern ceiling with soft built-in lighting, an extra room without major construction, or walls smooth enough to paint? This is the team for it.

  • Gypsum false ceilings with built-in and cove lighting
  • Room partitions for homes, offices and shops
  • Smooth plastering and wall crack repairs
  • Complete small fit-outs for shops and offices
