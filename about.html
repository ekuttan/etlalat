About Us

The neighbourhood team for every home job.

We're a local Dubai business — plumbers, painters, carpenters, AC technicians and tilers who take pride in their work and treat your home with respect.

Craftsmen, not contractors.

Most maintenance companies send whoever is free. We're different: every trade has its own specialist. The person who paints your wall is a painter. The person who fixes your AC is an AC technician. The person who builds your wardrobe has been a carpenter for years.

Because all our trades work under one roof, one call covers the whole job — the plumber, the tiler and the painter coordinate with each other, so your bathroom refit doesn't become three separate headaches.

How we work

You call or WhatsApp +971 50 128 4459 and describe the job — photos help. We give you a clear price and a visit time, usually within a few hours. Our craftsman arrives on time, protects your furniture and floors, does the work properly, and cleans up before leaving. The bill matches the quote.

Why people come back

No jargon, no upselling, no surprises — just skilled work at a fair price from people who care about doing it right. That's how we earned a 4.9-star rating from 876 customers across Dubai, and why most of our work comes from repeat customers and their neighbours.
